about me

I am a passionate computer science student focused on artificial intelligence. In my research I focus primary on the combination of deep learning with reinforcement learning. I am also a programmer with 8+ years of experience with several platforms (mainly .NET). In over 20 applications I designed and implemented, I focused on clean design following best practices for targeted platforms. The extensive knowledge of many programming languages and frameworks helped me to jump quickly into new technologies and choose the best architecture for new projects. To share my passion for new things with other people I started a new company called Softinum systems s.r.o.
