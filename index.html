about me

I am a passionate computer science student, specialized in artificial intelligence. I also work as a researcher and focus on deep reinforcement learning (DRL) and dialogue systems. In the DRL area, I am mainly interested in DRL visual navigation. Together with my fellow researchers, we published two papers about using auxiliary tasks to improve the PAAC algorithm's performance when applied to visual navigation. In the dialogue systems domain, I focus on combining task-oriented dialogue systems with pre-trained transformer-based language models, e.g., gpt2. I have always been an eager learner, and I always wanted to bring new ideas to the world and move humanity forward.
